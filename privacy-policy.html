Summary: Hidden Chronicles stores your game progress locally on your device only. We do not collect personal data, do not operate servers, and do not sell your information. The app may display ads from Google AdMob, which has its own data practices described below.

1. Who We Are

Hidden Chronicles: The Memory Detective is a mobile hidden-object game developed by AwardiDevTeam and published by WridaStore.

Contact Information Developer: AwardiDevTeam
Publisher: WridaStore
Email: wridastore@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide the game experience:

  • Detective Name: The username you choose, stored locally on your device via browser localStorage. Never transmitted to any server.
  • Game Progress: Levels completed, scores, coins, and daily reward data — stored locally only.
  • Preferences: Sound and theme settings stored locally on your device.

We do NOT collect: real name, email address, phone number, precise location, payment information, contacts, photos, microphone input, or any sensitive personal data.

3. How We Store Your Data

All game data is stored using localStorage — a standard web storage technology that keeps data entirely on your device. This data is never transmitted to servers operated by AwardiDevTeam or WridaStore. You can delete all stored data by clearing app data in your Android device settings (Settings → Apps → Hidden Chronicles → Clear Data).

4. Advertising (Google AdMob)

Hidden Chronicles may display advertisements served by Google AdMob and affiliated Google advertising partners. These services may collect and use certain information from your device to show relevant advertisements, including:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable device identifier)
  • IP address (used to determine approximate location at country/region level)
  • Device type, operating system version, and screen resolution
  • App usage data and ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

This data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Opt-out of personalized ads: You can reset your Advertising ID or opt out of ad personalization in your Android device settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or Opt out of Ads Personalization (earlier versions).

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Fonts: Font files are loaded from Google's CDN. Google may log metadata about these requests. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdMob: Advertising network. See Section 4 above.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app distribution, crash reporting, and device compatibility.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Hidden Chronicles is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through this app, please contact us immediately at wridastore@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

7. Your Rights (GDPR — EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Portability: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data for advertising

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can exercise the right to erasure directly by clearing app data. For advertising-related rights, please contact Google directly or use the device-level opt-out described in Section 4.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to: (1) know what personal information is collected, (2) know whether personal information is sold or disclosed, (3) opt out of the sale of personal information, (4) access their personal information, and (5) have their personal information deleted. We do not sell personal information. To exercise any rights, contact us at wridastore@gmail.com.

9. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security measures (PIN, biometric authentication, etc.). Because we do not transmit data to external servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach of your game information. We use standard web technologies (localStorage) that are sandboxed to our app only.

10. Data Retention

Game data is retained on your device until you clear it. If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data is deleted automatically by Android.

11. International Data Transfers

The game itself does not transfer data internationally. However, advertising partners (Google AdMob) may process data in the United States and other countries where Google operates. These transfers are subject to Google's data transfer mechanisms and the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.
